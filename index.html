About Us

Welcome to Tropical Tees N Things! Located in the heart of Grace Bay, Turks & Caicos, we're your one-stop shop for tropical clothing, thoughtful gifts, and authentic local treasures. Whether you're a visitor looking for the perfect island souvenir or a local seeking quality clothing and home goods, we've got you covered.

Our mission is to bring the island spirit to life through carefully curated products that celebrate the beauty, culture, and warmth of the Caribbean. Every item in our store tells a story of paradise.
